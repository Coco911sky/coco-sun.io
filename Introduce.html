Food for Spring Festival

During the Spring Festival, we eat dumplings, Tangyuan, New Year cake, wonton, long noodles, fish, chicken, etc. Every kind of food contains people's good expectations and blessings for the new year. However, China has a vast territory and abundant resources. The customs of different places are different, and the food eaten during the Spring Festival is also different.

Clothes for Spring Festival

1. The spring festival clothes like big red, especially for children. Wearing big red clothes means good luck, and Dao's life in the coming year will be prosperous. 2. New clothes must be worn during the Spring Festival. It is an old custom of Chinese people to wear new clothes during the Spring Festival. In the Song Dynasty, it is also recorded in the records of Dream China in Tokyo. The Song Dynasty was very interesting. It was a very prosperous period for the citizens. On the new year's day, everyone dressed clean and went around. So the new clothes almost became the brand of Spring Festival. Everyone wears new clothes after the new year. In Chinese tradition, wearing new clothes for the new year has a long history. Like dragon dance, lion dance, beating gongs and drums, pasting Spring Festival couplets, setting off firecrackers, and hanging lanterns, it is an indispensable part of the festival. The colorful new clothes for the Spring Festival set off a stronger and better festival atmosphere.

The activities of Spring Festival

Spring festival activities are more colorful. For example, fireworks, dragon dance, lion dance, flower market, even blind date.
